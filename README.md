## 🧠 About

I am a Malware Analyst focused on understanding how malicious software operates at a low level, with a strong emphasis on Linux-based threats and ransomware families.

My work revolves around analyzing ELF binaries, tracing execution flow, and studying how malware behaves in real-world scenarios — including evasion techniques, persistence mechanisms, and post-exploitation activity, especially in Linux server environments.

I am particularly interested in how modern threats target cloud-based infrastructures such as AWS, where scalability and misconfigurations introduce new attack surfaces.
